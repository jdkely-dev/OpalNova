# OPALNOVA V1.92.0 Operations Performance Reporting Testing Checklist

Use this checklist against the published V1.92 build.

- Launch OPALNOVA and confirm the header shows `Version 1.92.0 - operations performance reporting`.
- Open About and confirm it shows `Version 1.92.0 - Operations Performance Reporting`.
- Open `Reports`.
- Click `Operations Performance`.
- Confirm the report opens in the in-app preview.
- Confirm the report includes:
  - Operations Snapshot.
  - Workshop Productivity.
  - Supplier Diamond Performance.
  - Market Performance.
  - Suggested Report Rhythm.
- Open `Reports Studio`.
- Click `Operations Performance` again and confirm it generates the same style of report.
- Confirm the report does not create or edit tasks, jobs, sales, diamonds, markets or stock records.
- Confirm existing reports still open:
  - BI Command Report.
  - Weekly Sales.
  - Monthly Sales.
  - Market Report.
  - Production Capacity.
- Confirm `Open HTML`, `Print`, and report folder actions still work from the preview panel.
