# OPALNOVA V1.93.0 Inventory Reorder Intelligence Testing Checklist

Use this checklist against the published V1.93 build.

- Launch OPALNOVA and confirm the header shows `Version 1.93.0 - inventory reorder intelligence`.
- Open About and confirm it shows `Version 1.93.0 - Inventory Reorder Intelligence`.
- Open `Inventory`.
- Click `Inventory Intelligence`.
- Confirm the report opens in the in-app preview.
- Confirm the report includes:
  - Inventory Decision Snapshot.
  - Stock Lifecycle Guide.
  - Valuation By Category.
  - Low-Stock Reorder Recommendations.
  - Slow-Moving Stock Guidance.
  - Supplier Diamond Decision State.
  - Material Adjustment Audit.
  - Recommended Review Order.
- Open `Reports`.
- Click `Inventory Intelligence` and confirm it generates the same style of report.
- Open `Inventory Studio`.
- Click `Inventory Intelligence` and confirm it opens from that studio.
- Open `Reports Studio`.
- Click `Inventory Intelligence` and confirm it opens from the full report list.
- Confirm the report does not create or edit:
  - purchase orders,
  - stock quantities,
  - materials,
  - jewellery stock,
  - stones,
  - supplier diamonds,
  - tasks.
- Confirm existing inventory reports still open:
  - Inventory Value.
  - Stock Ageing.
  - Reserved Inventory.
  - Reorder Report.
  - Inventory Report.
- Confirm `Open HTML`, `Print`, and report folder actions still work from the preview panel.
