# OPALNOVA V1.84.0 Production Capacity Snapshot Testing Checklist

Use this checklist after launching the V1.84.0 build.

- [ ] Confirm the main header shows `Version 1.84.0 - production capacity snapshot`.
- [ ] Open About and confirm it shows `Version 1.84.0 - Production Capacity Snapshot`.
- [ ] Open Release Notes and confirm V1.84.0 appears above V1.83.0.
- [ ] Open Production Board.
- [ ] Click `Capacity Snapshot` and confirm the report opens in the in-app report preview.
- [ ] Confirm the report shows active job count, overdue count, due within 7 days, labour due within 7 days and missing labour-hour count.
- [ ] Confirm due-date buckets appear for overdue, due within 7 days, due 8-14 days and no due date.
- [ ] Confirm active jobs list with status, due date, labour hours, balance and planning note.
- [ ] Confirm active production batches appear when batches exist.
- [ ] Open Production workflow home and confirm `Capacity Snapshot` is available.
- [ ] Open Production & Opal Studio and confirm `Capacity Snapshot` is available.
- [ ] Open Reports Studio and confirm `Production Capacity` is available.
- [ ] Confirm opening the report does not change job statuses, payments, stock or production batches.
- [ ] Close OPALNOVA cleanly.
