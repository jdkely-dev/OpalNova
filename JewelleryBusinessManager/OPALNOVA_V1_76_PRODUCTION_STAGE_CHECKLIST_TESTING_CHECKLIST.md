# OPALNOVA V1.76.0 Production Stage Checklist Testing Checklist

Use this checklist after launching the V1.76.0 build.

- [ ] Confirm the main header shows `Version 1.76.0 - production stage checklist`.
- [ ] Open About and confirm it shows `Version 1.76.0 - Production Stage Checklist`.
- [ ] Open Production Board from the main workflow area.
- [ ] Select a job card and click `Stage Checklist`.
- [ ] Confirm the checklist opens in an OPALNOVA report tab.
- [ ] Confirm the report shows job code/title, current stage, due date and recommended next action.
- [ ] Confirm customer contact, linked quote and accepted option details are correct for a job with a linked quote.
- [ ] Confirm material, stone and supplier diamond rows appear when the accepted quote option has links.
- [ ] Confirm open linked tasks are listed and overdue tasks show as items to review.
- [ ] Confirm linked job photos/files are listed when photos are attached to the job.
- [ ] Confirm a job with no photos shows a clear review message rather than failing.
- [ ] Confirm a handover-stage job with a balance owing shows a payment waiting warning.
- [ ] Open Production studio and generate `Stage Checklist` from the selected-job setup panel.
- [ ] Open Production & Opal Studio and generate `Stage Checklist` from the selected-job setup panel.
- [ ] Open Documents Studio and generate `Production Stage Checklist` from the selected-job setup panel.
- [ ] Confirm existing `Move Forward`, `Move Back`, `Complete Selected` and `Edit Selected Job` actions still work.
- [ ] Confirm no records are created or changed by generating the checklist.
- [ ] Close the report tab and production board tab cleanly.
