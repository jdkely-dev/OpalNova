# OPALNOVA V1.46 Supplier Diamond Workflow Testing Checklist

1. Open OPALNOVA and confirm the dashboard loads normally.
2. Confirm the dashboard shows the new external diamond tiles.
3. Open **Diamond Supplier Studio**.
4. Open **Supplier Holds & Orders**.
5. Confirm saved external diamonds appear.
6. Select a linked external diamond from a quote option.
7. Enter a supplier reference and click **Request Hold**.
8. Confirm status changes to **Hold Requested**.
9. Set a hold expiry date and click **Confirm Hold**.
10. Confirm the dashboard hold count updates after refresh.
11. Click **Request / Mark Ordered** and set an expected arrival date.
12. Confirm status changes to **Ordered**.
13. Click **Create Reminder Task**.
14. Open Tasks and confirm the supplier diamond reminder exists.
15. Click **Mark Received** and confirm status changes to **Received**.
16. Open the related quote option and confirm the linked external diamond status is updated.
17. Generate a proposal and confirm the external diamond still appears correctly.
18. Publish and open OPALNOVA.exe.
