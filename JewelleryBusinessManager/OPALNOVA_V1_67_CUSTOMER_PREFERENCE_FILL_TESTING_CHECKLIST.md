# OPALNOVA V1.67.0 Customer Preference Fill Testing Checklist

Use this checklist after launching the V1.67.0 build.

- [ ] Confirm the main header shows `Version 1.67.0 - quote customer preference fill`.
- [ ] Open About and confirm it shows `Version 1.67.0 - Quote Customer Preference Fill`.
- [ ] Open or create a customer with Ring Sizes, Preferred Metals and Preferred Stones populated.
- [ ] Open Custom Quote Builder and select that customer.
- [ ] Leave Ring size, Preferred metal and Preferred stone blank, then click Use Customer Preferences.
- [ ] Confirm blank quote fields are filled from the customer profile.
- [ ] Enter a quote-specific value into one of those fields and click Use Customer Preferences again.
- [ ] Confirm existing quote-specific values are not overwritten.
- [ ] Confirm the quote is treated as unsaved after applying preferences by trying to close the tab.
- [ ] Save, reopen the quote, and confirm the copied context fields persist.
- [ ] Preview the proposal and confirm the customer-facing project details show the copied context.
- [ ] Close the app normally.
