# OPALNOVA V1.96.0 Workspace Surface Reduction Testing Checklist

Use this checklist against the published V1.96 build.

- Launch OPALNOVA and confirm the header shows `Version 1.96.0 - workspace surface reduction`.
- Open About and confirm it shows `Version 1.96.0 - Workspace Surface Reduction`.
- Open Alert Centre and confirm the header, metric row and selected-alert panel are compact but still readable.
- Open Project Workbench and confirm the metric cards take less vertical space and the project grid/details remain usable.
- Open Proposal Pipeline and confirm the proposal grid and action panel have more room without losing action buttons.
- Open Payment & Collection and confirm the header, selected job summary and money cards are compact and readable.
- Open Production Board and confirm the title/filter/footer areas take less vertical space while board lanes remain accessible.
- Open Supplier Holds & Orders and confirm filters, counters, grid and selected diamond actions remain visible and usable.
- Open Stock Movement and confirm the form is tighter while all required inputs and caution text remain present.
- Confirm no workflow action creates or edits records just by opening these screens.
- Confirm Debug build, Release publish and published launch smoke have passed before treating this as release-ready.
