# OPALNOVA V1.80.0 Stability Milestone Testing Checklist

Use this checklist after launching the V1.80.0 build.

- [ ] Confirm the main header shows `Version 1.80.0 - stability milestone`.
- [ ] Open About and confirm it shows `Version 1.80.0 - Stability Milestone`.
- [ ] Open Release Notes and confirm V1.80.0 appears above V1.79.0.
- [ ] Open the dashboard and confirm there is only one Recent Work panel.
- [ ] Open Production Board and confirm Stage Checklist is available and generates a report for a selected job.
- [ ] Open Payment & Collection and confirm there is only one Payment Schedule panel for the selected job.
- [ ] Confirm Record Payment, Generate Invoice / Receipt, Copy Balance Reminder and handover actions still appear once each.
- [ ] Open Custom Quote Builder and confirm quote totals still show schedule guidance without blocking normal proposal preview/send workflow.
- [ ] Generate a job payment summary and confirm the payment schedule appears before the payment history ledger.
- [ ] Open Change Inventory Status for jewellery stock or a stone and confirm lifecycle guidance appears before saving.
- [ ] Generate Inventory Value, Stock Ageing, Reserved Inventory and Opal / Stone Stock reports and confirm lifecycle guidance appears without editing records.
- [ ] Open Supplier Diamond Holds & Orders and confirm the lifecycle column appears once.
- [ ] Confirm supplier diamond hold/order/received/convert actions still appear once each.
- [ ] Open Backup / Data Safety and confirm backup, restore preview, health check and release notes actions still work.
- [ ] Confirm no payment record, stock movement, reservation, sale or supplier diamond status changes just by opening guidance or reports.
- [ ] Close the app cleanly.
