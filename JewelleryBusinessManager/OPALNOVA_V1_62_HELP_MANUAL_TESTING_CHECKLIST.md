# OPALNOVA V1.62.0 Help Manual Testing Checklist

Use this checklist against the published V1.62 build.

## Startup

- Launch OPALNOVA.
- Confirm the header shows `Version 1.62.0 - built-in help manual refresh`.
- Open About and confirm it shows V1.62.0.

## User Guide Entry Points

- Open Settings & Backup.
- Click User Guide.
- Confirm the guide opens in the default browser/app.
- Open Safety & Data Studio.
- Click User Guide.
- Confirm the same guide opens.

## Guide Content

- Confirm the guide title reads `OPALNOVA User Guide`.
- Confirm the contents section is visible.
- Confirm the guide includes:
  - setup and daily rhythm.
  - quotes and proposals.
  - production workflow.
  - payments, invoices and handover.
  - inventory and supplier diamonds.
  - reports and bookkeeping review.
  - backups, restore and data safety.
  - release testing routine.

## Safety Warnings

- Confirm the quote acceptance caution is visible.
- Confirm the job completion/material consumption caution is visible.
- Confirm the supplier stock caution is visible.
- Confirm restore/import/bulk update caution is visible.

## Readability

- Confirm the guide has no obvious corrupted punctuation in the title or section headers.
- Confirm the guide prints or previews cleanly.
- Confirm the guide remains readable at normal browser zoom.

## Safety

- Confirm opening the guide does not add, edit or delete records.
- Close and reopen OPALNOVA.
- Confirm existing records and reports still load normally.
