# OPALNOVA V1.66.0 Unsaved Change Testing Checklist

Use this checklist after launching the V1.66.0 build.

- [ ] Confirm the main header shows `Version 1.66.0 - quote unsaved change guard`.
- [ ] Open About and confirm it shows `Version 1.66.0 - Quote Unsaved Change Guard`.
- [ ] Open Custom Quote Builder in a workspace tab.
- [ ] Change the quote title, context fields, customer notes or internal notes, then click the tab close button.
- [ ] Confirm OPALNOVA prompts to Save, Discard or Cancel.
- [ ] Choose Cancel and confirm the quote tab remains open.
- [ ] Click close again, choose Discard and confirm the tab closes.
- [ ] Reopen Custom Quote Builder, change a field, click New Quote and confirm the same prompt appears.
- [ ] Choose Save from the prompt and confirm the quote saves before the new quote starts.
- [ ] Change an option cost, recommendation, linked stone/material/external diamond or design image and confirm tab close prompts.
- [ ] Save manually, then close the tab and confirm no prompt appears.
- [ ] Preview Proposal or Send / Record after editing and confirm the dirty prompt does not appear immediately after successful save.
- [ ] Close the app normally.
