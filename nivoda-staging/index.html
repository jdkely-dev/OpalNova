Implemented in OPALNOVA

Current local workflow surface.

  • Authentication diagnostics
    User-entered credentials can be tested from the local app and are never placed on this page.
  • Diamond search mapping
    Supplier id, certificate, shape, carat, colour, clarity, cut, lab, price, availability, image/video and certificate fields are mapped where present.
  • Supplier stock separation
    Saved external diamonds remain separate from owned stock until physically received and converted.
  • Hold/order mutations
    API-backed actions remain disabled until Nivoda confirms accessible mutation names and required payloads.

Needs Nivoda Confirmation

Items required before live order actions.

  • Current staging and production endpoint URLs.
  • Search query and filter names for natural/lab, shape, carat, lab, price, availability, certificate and video fields.
  • Account access for hold, reserve, cart, order, purchase or checkout mutations.
  • Required payloads for holds, orders, price refresh, availability refresh and supplier order documents.
  • Token lifetime, rate limit, region, permission and supplier visibility constraints.
  • Whether a webhook or callback URL is required for this desktop app workflow.
